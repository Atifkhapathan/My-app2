Our Services

dRegular bus service between Pune and Pimpri-Chinchwad is the main mode of transport between both the city limits. Carrying 10 to 11 lakh passengers daily, it is the most reliable mode of transport for all Punekars. PMPML's buses are known for their punctuality and efficient service on the busiest routes from 05.30 am to 12.00 am. At present, services have reached PMRDA boundary in the Pune and Pimpri-Chinchwad area covering a radius of 40 km with 389 routes. Also, based on the suggestions received frequently and by studying the population of the city, expansion plans are drawn up and new ones are implemented. About 21916 trips across 1660 schedules are scheduled for daily bus travel today and approximately 96% of the operations are efficiently serving passengers.
